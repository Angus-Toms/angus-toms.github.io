OUR
STORY

Founded in 2024, Edinburgh Bike Fitting RT emerged from a simple vision: to combine competitive racing excellence with professional bike fitting expertise. What started as a local initiative has grown into a formidable presence on the Scottish racing circuit.

Our first season has been nothing short of remarkable, with 4 national titles, 19 race victories, and 75 top-ten finishes establishing us as a force to be reckoned with in Scottish cycling.
